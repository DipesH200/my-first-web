Quantum Lab Builds is a personal experimental space for building things that do not exist yet. Every project starts as a question nobody is asking — and ends as something real.

From real-time global conflict tracking to unconventional software architectures, the lab operates at the intersection of technology, curiosity, and a refusal to do things the boring way.

Nothing here is finished. Everything here is alive.
